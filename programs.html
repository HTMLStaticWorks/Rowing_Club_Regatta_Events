Start Your Rowing Journey

From absolute beginners to elite competitors, we have a program for you.

Program Levels

01

Beginner / Learn-to-Row

A comprehensive 6-week introduction to rowing. No prior experience required. You'll learn the basics of the stroke, water safety training, and boat handling. By the end, you'll be rowing confidently in a crew.

02

Intermediate / Development

An 8-week program focusing on technique refinement and building endurance. Requires completion of the Beginner course. You'll transition into finer boats and start integrating piece work.

03

Advanced / Competitive

A 10-week intensive race preparation program. Selection-based entry. Focus on competitive speed, race strategy, and peak performance targeting the upcoming regatta season.
