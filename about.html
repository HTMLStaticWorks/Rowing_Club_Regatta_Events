Rowing Since 1987 — Our River Story

Built on passion, competition, and a deep respect for the water.

Our Mission

To promote the sport of rowing in a safe, inclusive, and highly competitive environment. We strive to provide our members with the best equipment, coaching, and facilities, enabling them to reach their full potential on and off the water.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the premier rowing club in the region, fostering a lifelong love for river sports. We aim to host world-class regattas and develop athletes who compete at national and international levels while maintaining our strong community roots.

Club History

1987

Club Founded

Started by a group of five local enthusiasts with just two wooden sculls operating out of a shared tin shed.

1995

First Boathouse Built

Secured funding to build the original timber boathouse, expanding our fleet to 15 boats.

2010

National Champions

The Men's Open Eight won gold at the National Championships, putting RiverPulse on the map.

2025

Modern Expansion

Opening our new state-of-the-art training facility and hosting the largest regional regatta to date.
